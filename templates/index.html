System Information

Getting Started

Click the Start Listening button or the STT button to begin voice interaction. Ensure your microphone is enabled.

Voice Controls

Choose from Female, Male, or Robotic voices. Adjust speech speed from Slow to Fast using the slider.

Language Support

Supports English, isiZulu, Sesotho, Afrikaans, isiXhosa, Setswana, and Kiswahili for both STT and Translation.

Translation

Enable real-time translation by selecting a target language. Your speech will be translated automatically.

Text-to-Speech

Type any text in the input field at the bottom of the conversation panel and click "Speak" to hear it aloud.

Conversation Management

Save conversations to file, clear history, or load previous conversations using the control buttons.

System Power

Use the power switch to connect or disconnect from the server. The status indicator shows connection state.

Audio Visualizer

Watch the waveform animate while recording. The audio level meter shows real-time input volume.

Tips & Tricks

Speak clearly and at a normal pace. Use headphones to avoid feedback. Check connection status before recording.
